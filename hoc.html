Hour of Code project

From booking a taxi or ordering food to manufacturing cars or planes, coding plays more and more crucial role in our lives,
especially given the current circumstances related to pandemic.
Unfortunately, we feel that in Azerbaijan, unlike in other more developed countries, people appreciate the importance
of programming much less than they should. Hence, we, students of #ADAUniversity, thought that it is our duty to
educate younger generation and decided to organize the #HourOfCode event
to introduce them to the World of Coding.
